Sustainability & Environmental Stewardship

Comprehensive environmental approach with native-only landscaping and maximum ecosystem stewardship

Hawks Ridge implements a zero-tolerance approach to non-native landscaping, making it one of the first major developments in Georgia to commit to 100% native species only.
